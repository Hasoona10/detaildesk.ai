Frequently asked questions.

What is DetailDesk AI?

A voice AI that picks up your business calls, handles the conversation, captures the lead, and texts you a summary. Works 24/7. Trained on your services and pricing.

Do I have to change my phone number?

No. Keep your number and forward calls on busy/no-answer, after hours, or full-time — you choose. The AI only answers what you'd otherwise miss.

How does it handle pricing questions?

It quotes your real price ranges (e.g. "ceramic coating typically runs $800–$1,500 depending on paint condition") and never invents exact prices. Anything uncertain becomes a captured lead for you to call back.

Is call recording legal in California?

California is an all-party consent state (Penal Code §632). DetailDesk AI's greeting discloses up front that the call may be recorded and handled by an AI assistant, so callers consent by continuing. We set this up correctly for you by default.

What about texting customers?

Summaries texted to you (the owner) are transactional. Any texting to your customers follows TCPA consent rules — the AI only texts callers who ask for a link or opt in, and every thread honors STOP/HELP.

How fast is setup?

Under a day. We're local — for LA/OC shops we'll do the setup with you, in person if you want.
